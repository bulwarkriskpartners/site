HERO IMAGE INSTRUCTIONS
=======================

To add a hero background image:

1. Add your hero image as: /public/hero.jpg
2. Recommended dimensions: 1920x1080px or larger
3. Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
4. The image should be professional and relevant to risk management/finance

If no hero image is provided, the site will show a gradient background.

Current fallback: Dark gradient from gray-900 to black to gray-800
